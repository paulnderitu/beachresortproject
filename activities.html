ACTIVITIES

Hilton Nairobi, located in the central business district, presents a well-equipped modern spa and health club with amenities to soothe the mind, body and soul and maintain fitness. We offer a variety of treatments to help you relax and refresh.

The spa features therapies that pamper and rejuvenate. From body scrubs to reflexology, treatments include aromatherapy, exfoliation, massage and hot stones to leave you free of tension, yet invigorated.

Hilton Health Club offers personal trainer services, Zumba and aerobics classes, steam/sauna and a heated outdoor pool. Membership provides access to the health club and spa, with body treatments charged at an additional fee.

Free parking is provided for all members using the spa and health club. Membership also includes voluntary opt-in to engaging outdoor activities such as annual charity marathon races, day hikes and expeditions, among others. An additional fee applies for these events.
